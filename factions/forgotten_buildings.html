Forgotten Infrastructure

The Living Architecture of the Empire
The Empire of the Forgotten does not build with stone or steel. Its structures are grown, not constructed — extensions of the Mother Tree's will, coaxed from the soil over decades or conjured in a single night of Malicott's attention. Each building is a vessel of memory, a shaped intention that guides a reborn soul further along the path the Mother has seen within them. To erect one is to make a promise to the future.
