The Imperial Core

The Valkyrion Empire was born from a Founder who realized that honor cannot precede reason. They strive to be just, even fully knowing the world will not allow it. Their existence is a constant, necessary compromise between their moral compass and their brutal survival.

At the pinnacle of this civilization stands the Emperor, a demi-god figure who inherits the Will of the Founder and exercises absolute mastery over the soul. The Emperor does not merely rule; they act as the spiritual and neurological anchor for the entire Empire, binding the collective will into a singular, undeniable force.

Tactical Doctrine

On the battlefield, the Empire operates as a cohesive, unbreakable unit. Their forces boast a highly balanced roster characterized by exceptional healing support and divine arts. By design and doctrine, they are unparalleled experts in the eradication of demonic entities, bringing the light of reason and the crushing weight of steel to the darkest corners of the world.
