Forgotten Spells

The Living Art of the Root Mother
The spells of the Empire of the Forgotten are not cast in the heat of battle — they are spoken in stillness, in the hush between marches, beneath the canopy of ancient trees. They are remembered: each incantation a fragment of Malicott's will, breathed into those she has touched and kept alive against all odds. They speak of roots that refuse to be torn, of sap that flows even through dying wood, of the stubborn, aching desire to endure. To invoke them is not to command power — it is to ask the earth whether it is willing to hold you a little longer.
