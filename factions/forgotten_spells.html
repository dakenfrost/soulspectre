Forgotten Spells

The Living Art of the Root Mother
The spells of the Empire of the Forgotten are not learned — they are remembered. Each incantation is a fragment of the Mother Tree's will, channelled through Malicott and passed down to those she has granted a second life. They speak in roots, in spores, in the slow patience of ancient wood. To cast them is to borrow from the earth something it will eventually take back.
