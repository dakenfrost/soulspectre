Valkyrion Empire

Disillusioned knights fighting for what they believe is right.

The Valkyrion Empire was born from a Founder who realized that honor cannot precede reason. They strive to be just, even fully knowing the world will not allow it. Their existence is a constant, necessary compromise between their moral compass and their brutal survival.

Lore

The Night of Tragedy, the Founder's Will, and the Dark Years.

Culture

The pragmatic morality and the burden of those who come after.

Units

The Four Orders: Sword, Light, Knowledge, and Engineering.
