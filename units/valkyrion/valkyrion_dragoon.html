Dragoon

Champion of the Order - Tier 5

The Iron Annihilator

Master engineers who perfect all known technologies develop heavily customized weapons and armor, transcending the standard ranks. These imposing soldiers are the pinnacle of Valkyrion's military-industrial complex.

Wielding massive portable cannons, aptly named Dragons, and encased in reinforced mechanical armor, Dragoons devastate the battlefield. They are slow and methodical, but when they fire, the sheer kinetic shockwave is enough to level enemy formations.
