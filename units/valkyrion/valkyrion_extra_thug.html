Thug

Valkyrion Empire - Tier 2 Underworld Enforcer

The Brutal Enforcer

Thugs are the heavy muscle of the Valkyrion undercity and fringe settlements. Lacking the discipline of the imperial military and the finesse of the criminal elite, they rely entirely on raw, unadulterated brute strength to shatter obstacles and intimidate the local populace.

Armed with heavy hammers, maces, or makeshift bludgeons, their combat approach is entirely devoid of technique. They exist to absorb punishment and deliver bone-breaking trauma, making them highly effective at dismantling defensive lines through sheer, concussive force.

Hammer Smash

Base Skill
A slow but devastating swing of a heavy blunt weapon. Deals raw Physical Damage to a single melee target.

Crushing Blow

Active Skill
Channels raw momentum into a devastating overhead strike. Deals 150% Physical Damage to a melee target. The sheer concussive force shatters physical defenses, ignoring up to 10% of the target's Armor.
