Captain

Valkyrion Empire - Tier 5 Support

The Urban Sovereign

Effective rulers of the Valkyrion Empire's vast metropolises, Captains are the physical embodiment of imperial law and administrative might. Their authority is absolute within their domain, backed not only by political influence but by immense personal power.

Combining profound knowledge, unbreakable charisma, and a terrifying mastery of strategy, an Captain rarely needs to draw a weapon to win a battle. However, when pushed to the frontline, they manipulate the flow of combat, commanding subordinates to die for them and crushing dissent with overwhelming force. They are not just opponents; they are the system itself fighting back.

Devastating Slash

Base Skill
Executes a brutal melee strike dealing Physical Damage. This calculated blow ignores 20% of the target's Armor and has a 50% chance to inflict Stun, leaving the enemy defenseless.

Kill Order

Active Skill
Issues an irrevocable imperial mandate, inflicting the Marked for Death debuff on a single target for 2 turns. The marked enemy suffers 50% increased damage from all incoming attacks.

Ruler

Passive
The Captain's absolute authority commands the battlefield. Gains 5% Damage Reduction for every active allied unit in the party. Simultaneously inspires the ranks, granting all other allies a +5% bonus to Armor and Damage.

Summon Reinforcements

Ultimate
Calls upon the empire's finest to crush opposition. Instantly summons two Honor Guards to the Captain's side to intercept threats and bolster the imperial formation.
