Squire

Order of the Sword - Tier 1

Fundamentals of Combat

The entry rank for all who wish to join the Order of Swords. Squires are equipped with chainmail and taught the fundamentals of combat.

They are well-balanced units, with solid defense, offense, and speed. While they lack the specialized skills of higher-tier units, their adaptability forms the backbone of the Valkyrion infantry lines.
