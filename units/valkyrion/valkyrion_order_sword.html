Order of the Sword

Protectors & Purgers

Melee Vanguard

The Order of Swords specializes in melee combat and consists of resilient frontline fighters. After graduating from school, an individual may join the Order as a Squire, the entry rank of the Order. Once a Squire gains sufficient experience, they may advance either into the Knighthood or the Inquisition.

Knights are dedicated to protecting civilians and punishing evildoers. Trained to wear heavy armor, they specialize either in offense or defense, depending on whether their spirit is more inclined toward judgment or protection.

Members of the Inquisition, on the other hand, focus on enemies from within: witches, monsters, and heretics who hide among the population and conspire against the Empire. While they lack the refined martial techniques of Knights, they compensate with specialized defenses and highly effective attacks against such threats.

Warriors who distinguish themselves through experience, leadership, and prowess may be personally selected by the Empress or Emperor and granted the title of Hero, serving directly under the Ruler and leading other units into battle.
