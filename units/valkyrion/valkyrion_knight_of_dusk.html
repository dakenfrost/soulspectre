Knight of Dusk

Champion of the Order - Tier 5

The Unyielding Light

Rare individuals who have mastered the righteous fury of the Holy Avengers, the steadfast defense of the Guardians, and the mental discipline of the Inquisitors may rise as Knights of Dusk.

They are the final hope when the darkest hour arrives—an unyielding light forged in shadow, not radiant, but relentless. They do not fight to change the world; they fight to keep the world from changing them.

Grim Determination

Oblivion
