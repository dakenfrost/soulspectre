Revenant

Valkyrion Empire - Tier 5 Necromantic Summon

The Undying Colossus

Classified as a high-risk necromantic anomaly, Revenants are massive, resurrected entities bound to the material plane through sheer, uncompromising spite. Summoning one requires specialized dark rituals, pulling an ancient warlord from the abyss to serve as an unbreakable vanguard.

A Revenant does not process pain or fatigue. Completely immune to psychic interference, toxins, and incineration, it operates as an engine of attrition. The more physical damage its colossal frame sustains, the more violent its necrotic output becomes, draining the life force of entire enemy formations to mend its shattered armor.

Vengeful Flames

Base Skill
Unleashes a torrent of necrotic ignition. Deals Dark Damage across an entire enemy group simultaneously.

Draining Flames

Active Skill
Initiates a widespread parasitic purge. Deals 50% of standard Dark Damage to all enemies in a targeted group. The Revenant mechanically absorbs the stolen vitality, healing itself for 100% of the total damage dealt.

Restless Spirit

Passive
The entity's lethality scales inversely with its structural integrity. Total Damage output is increased by 1% for every 3% of missing Health. Additionally, the volatile magical frame detonates upon impact, dealing 10 Fire Damage in direct retaliation to any attacker that lands a hit.
