Inquisitor

Order of the Sword - Tier 4

The Soul Binder

Witch Hunters who have endured the darkest horrors life can offer may become Inquisitors. These individuals possess exceptional mental fortitude and have mastered forbidden arts, binding their enemies in preparation for final judgment.

They are the silent terror of the Empire's enemies, capable of silencing a soul before the blade even touches the flesh. Their existence is a constant reminder that no heresy can hide from the Emperor's gaze.
