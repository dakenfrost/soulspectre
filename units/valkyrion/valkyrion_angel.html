Angel

Champion of the Order - Tier 5

The Mortal Ascendant

Clerics who master all of God’s ways transcend their mortal limits and become full-fledged Angels. These divine beings are exceedingly rare, existing as the ultimate messengers of the Father's will on earth.

Wielding spears of pure light and radiating an aura of absolute sanctity, they are said to work literal miracles for mankind, defending the innocent and obliterating the profane with a single thought.

Tier5 ★
Hit Points250

Heaven Divider

Base Skill
The spear unleashes a colossal beam. Deals 100% of Attack as Holy Damage to all units in a line.

Absolute Light

Active Skill
Deals Light damage to nearby enemies. Unholy creatures (Devils, Demons, Undead) are Blinded and Burning. Heals and buffs nearby allies with +10% Dodge.

Savior

Passive
Counters dodged melee attacks with Heaven Divider. Doubles damage vs Profane and halves damage taken from them. Automatically Heals allies below 50% HP (max 2/turn).

Miracle

Ultimate
The ultimate divine intervention. Heals all allies for 50% Max HP, Resurrects dead allies at 50% HP, and Removes all debuffs.
