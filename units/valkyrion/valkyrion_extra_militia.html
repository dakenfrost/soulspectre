Militia

Valkyrion Empire - Tier 1 Patrol Unit

The Common Guard

The Militia forms the rudimentary law enforcement and border patrol network of the Valkyrion Empire. Recruited primarily from the peasantry and stationed in towns or minor outposts, they are the first and weakest line of imperial defense.

They are provided with only the most basic rudimentary training and equipped with standard-issue gear manufactured for cost-efficiency rather than maximum survivability. While unsuited for heavy combat against seasoned adversaries, they serve their purpose by maintaining local order and stalling minor threats through sheer numbers until regular military forces arrive.
