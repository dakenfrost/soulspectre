Magus

Champion of the Order - Tier 5

The Arcane Duality

Driven by an insatiable thirst for absolute power, the Magus seeks to master both scholastic spellcasting and natural magic. Achieved through extreme esoteric experimentation, they control a male and a female body simultaneously.

Though they appear as twins on the battlefield, they are fundamentally a single soul split across two physical forms. This terrifying duality allows them to bypass conventional magical limitations, unleashing a seamless, devastating barrage of structured destruction and primal debilitation.

Combat Interface

Tier5 ★
Hit Points250
Attack80
Initiative45

Pyroclasm

Base Skill
Deals Fire damage to enemies in a group. Applies Burn for 1 turn (deals 50% of attack at the start of the afflicted unit's turn).

Lightning Storm

Skill
Deals Air damage equal to 150% of attack to a group with a 40% chance to stun. Applies the "Shocked" debuff for 2 turns (units take 20% more damage and have a 10% chance to be stunned on their turn).

Magic Mastery

Passive
Targets afflicted by Magus debuffs deal 10% less damage. All Magus skills and debuffs bypass Resistance to Fire, Dark, and Primal damage. At the start of each combat turn (excluding its own) gains 50 Shield.

Primal Explosion

Ultimate
A cataclysmic burst of raw energy. Deals Primal damage equal to 200% of attack to enemies in a group. Applies the "Silence" debuff for 1 turn (prevents the use of active skills).
