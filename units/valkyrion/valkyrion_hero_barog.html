Chloé

The Shy Witch | Tier 2 Adept Hero

Gentle Soul, Hidden Power

"The flowers don't care about the Empire... why can't we be more like them? Please, just let me go back to my garden."

Formerly a promising talent within the imperial academies, Chloé fled the rigid structure of Valkyrion to find peace in the countryside. Her magic is instinctive and deeply tied to the natural world, though she possesses a dramatic, shadowed past that she refuses to discuss.

Hit Points140
Attack35
Initiative60
ImmunityMind

Combat Doctrine

Magic Bolt

Base

Unleashes a concentrated orb of raw arcane energy. Deals standard magic damage to a single enemy at range.

Soul Hex

Active

Chloé's fear manifests as a curse. Deals magic damage to a target and reduces their Attack by 20% for 2 turns.

Forest Veil

Passive

Her shy nature makes her hard to target. Chloé gains an innate +15% Dodge chance against all ranged attacks.
