Barog

The Grim Hunter | Tier 2 Adept

"Guilty or innocent, it matters not. Barog would rather see 100 innocents burn than let an evil witch get free; every price is not too high to deliver to god's mercy the spawns of evil. The impurity must be purged by iron and silence."

Hit Points 160
Attack 50
Initiative 50
Special Immunity: Mind

Combat Doctrine

Base Skill

Slash

Deals standard physical damage to a single enemy in melee range.

Active Skill

Execution

Deals high physical damage to a single enemy in melee range. Damage increases by 1% for every 1% of the target's missing HP.

Passive Trait

Blind Justice

Barog's fanatical hatred for the arcane grants him a 30% damage bonus against all Caster classes.
