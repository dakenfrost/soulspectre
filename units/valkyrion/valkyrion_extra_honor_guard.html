Honor Guard

Valkyrion Empire - Tier 4 Support

The Unyielding Sentinel

Hand-picked from the surviving veterans of a hundred campaigns, the Honor Guard represent the absolute zenith of Valkyrion military discipline. Clad in impenetrable imperial steel and armed with master-crafted weaponry, they are elite sentinels who answer only to the highest echelons of command.

In combat, an Honor Guard functions as an unbreakable anchor for the imperial formation. Their tactical doctrine revolves around absorbing punishing blows meant for their superiors and demoralizing the enemy vanguard through sheer, inexorable presence. To face an Honor Guard is to strike against the very walls of the Empire itself.

Intimidating Slash

Base Skill
Executes a calculated melee strike dealing Physical Damage to an enemy. The sheer brutality of the blow applies the Attack Down debuff, demoralizing the target and reducing their total damage output by 10% for 2 turns.

Defensive Position

Active Skill
The Honor Guard locks their shield and roots themselves into the battlefield, adopting a Defensive Stance for 3 turns. While active, all incoming damage taken by the unit is reduced by 30%.

Bodyguard

Passive
A sworn protector of the imperial line. Passively intercepts and absorbs 50% of all damage directed at adjacent allies (stationed behind or at their sides). Furthermore, their supreme conditioning allows them to regenerate 15% of their Maximum HP at the start of every turn.
