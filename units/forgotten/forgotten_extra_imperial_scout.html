Imperial Scout

Tier 2 · Hostile Unit

Eyes of the Empire

They move in pairs or threes, clad in muted colors chosen for the terrain. Their orders are precise: map the boundaries, identify points of weakness, note the location and strength of any Forgotten presence encountered. Then eliminate it before reporting back.

Imperial Scouts receive specialized training in the suppression of nature-bound units. They carry amber-disrupting compounds that temporarily destabilize a Forgotten's connection to the forest — enough to slow a pursuit, enough to ensure the report reaches its destination.

They do not believe in what they hunt. They believe in the mission, the map, and the empire that will reward completion. To the Forgotten, that indifference is its own kind of cruelty — worse, in some ways, than hatred.
