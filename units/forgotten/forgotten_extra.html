Hostile Units

Poacher

Classification: Tier 1

Opportunistic hunters who stray into sacred forest territory, drawn by the rarity of its creatures. They lack any respect for the life that Malicott has cultivated, and the roots do not forget trespassers.

Data

Deserter

Classification: Tier 1

Imperial soldiers who fled the purges and took refuge in the wilderness. Starving and desperate, they have abandoned discipline for survival, attacking anything they encounter on the forest edge.

Data

Imperial Scout

Classification: Tier 2

Valkyrion reconnaissance specialists dispatched to map the borders of Forgotten territory. They travel light, report swiftly, and are ordered to eliminate any Forgotten presence they encounter rather than risk exposure.

Data

Defector

Classification: Tier 2

Elves who rejected Malicott's pact and fled the empire. Unable to fully return to mortal life and unwilling to acknowledge what they are, they exist in a bitter in-between — hostile to the Forgotten, despised by the world beyond the forest.

Data

Witch Hunter

Classification: Tier 3

Veteran soldiers trained specifically to track and neutralize entities bound to nature magic. They carry amber-breaking tools and blessed blades designed to sever the connection between a Forgotten and the Mother Tree's will.

Data

Crimson Soldier

Classification: Tier 3

Elite infantry dispatched during the Crimson Campaign — the empire's most ruthless attempt to eradicate the Forgotten. Clad in red-lacquered armor, they march under orders that leave no room for mercy or retreat.

Data

Inquisitor

Classification: Tier 4

Religious and military authority combined into a single lethal figure. Inquisitors view the Forgotten as an abomination against the natural order of death — a belief that Malicott's empire has spent centuries proving wrong, one battle at a time.

Data

Crimson Champion

Classification: Tier 5

The personal vanguard of the Crimson Campaign's high command. A Crimson Champion has never known defeat — until the Forgotten. They carry the weight of every burning forest and every silenced root as a badge of pride. Malicott remembers each one by name.

Data

Summoned Units

Root Echoes

Classification: Tier 1 · Non-Playable

Fragments of life that have not yet chosen a form. When Malicott calls them through the Summon Wisps spell, Root Echoes drift into the world as extensions of her will — not soldiers, not scouts, but living proof that the forest is always present.

Data

Voice of the Root

Classification: Tier 5 · Non-Playable

The oldest presence Malicott can call forth. When the Ancient Growth spell reaches its full resonance, the Voice of the Root walks beside the army — not to fight, but to remind everything alive that the Mother Tree predates every war ever waged against her.
