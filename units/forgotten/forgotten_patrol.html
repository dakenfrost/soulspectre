Patrol

Officer , Arboreal Fighters

Origin Unknown

No one knows where the Patrols come from. Even other Arborei, who share the language of root and etheric vibration, cannot identify which battlefield they remember, which war etched itself into their bark. They arrived in the Empire already formed, already commanding. Malicott did not create them , she recognized them.

What distinguishes a Patrol from other officers in the Empire is a quality that cannot be taught: the ability to be present. While other commanders plan from the rear, Patrols stand in the thick of the formation, their feet planted like ancient roots, their eyes tracking every movement on the field. They speak little. When they do speak, everyone listens , because they speak only what is necessary, and what is necessary is always true.

Among the undead warriors of the Empire, the Patrols hold a special authority. The Arborei feel an instinctive solidarity with them, a resonance they describe as "hearing the same memory." In battle, when a Patrol raises a fist in the signal to attack, the dead move before the living can register the command.

Attack

Base Attack
Deals Physical melee damage to a single target. If that enemy is eliminated during the same turn, all allied units receive a +10 Morale boost, granting temporary resistance to fear and routing.

Ethereal Coordination

Active
All allied units gain +20% Precision and their attacks ignore 15% of enemy Armor for 2 turns. Units already at maximum Precision instead gain a +10% critical hit chance for the duration. The Patrol's own strikes also pierce magical barriers during this window.

Undeath Resonance

Passive
Undead allied units deal +10% Critical Damage. Additionally, each time an enemy unit is killed anywhere on the battlefield, all allied units recover 5% of their maximum HP. Death is not a loss , death is a resource, and the Patrol ensures none is wasted.

Emerald Assault

Ultimate
All allied units simultaneously attack a random enemy of the Patrol's choosing, overwhelming target positioning. For 2 turns after, the entire army benefits from +15% Initiative and +10% Critical Chance on all physical attacks. The emerald surge: the moment when the dead remember war.
