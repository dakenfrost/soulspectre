Defector

Tier 2 · Hostile Unit

Between Two Deaths

There is no name in the empire's records for what they are. The Forgotten call them Defectors — elves who, at some point in the long history of Malicott's pact, chose to walk away from it. They refused the second life. They tried to return to mortality, to age, to the ordinary darkness of a life that ends.

The forest does not allow clean exits. Malicott does not pursue them, but the sap in their veins does not simply drain away. Defectors exist in a permanent in-between: too alive to die quietly, too separated from the root network to heal naturally. They are bitter in the way that only half-things can be bitter — against the empire that cannot accept them, and against the empire of the Forgotten that they chose to leave.

They attack both. They belong to neither. And the forest watches them with something that, if pressed, Malicott might describe as grief.
