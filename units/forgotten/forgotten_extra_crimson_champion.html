Crimson Champion

Tier 5 · Hostile Unit

The Undefeated

There is a record kept in the high command of the Crimson Campaign: a list of battles, each annotated with outcome and casualty count. Every entry attributed to a Crimson Champion ends the same way. Before the Forgotten.

They are the personal vanguard of the Campaign's highest authority — selected not just for martial excellence but for ideological purity. A Crimson Champion has internalized the campaign's mandate so completely that they no longer require orders. They understand, at a level beneath language, what the empire wants the world to look like. And they have spent their entire adult life moving it toward that image.

Malicott remembers each of them by name. Not out of fear — she does not fear them — but because she knows that things which move with that much purpose leave marks, and marks are worth tracking. The forest forgets nothing. The Champions count on the forest forgetting everything. That is the first of their miscalculations.
