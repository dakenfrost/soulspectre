Shadows of the Sap

Surgeons of the Soul

The Pain is Only Information

The Shadows of the Sap are the field surgeons and life distributors of the Empire , custodians of its vital reserves. However, in the pragmatic framework of the Forgotten, "healing" is not a gentle act. It is a brutal redistribution of vital resources.

Malicott creates these beings not from solid wood or ash, but using her own Sap, interwoven with the shadow matter typical of ghosts. The result is a humanoid creature that appears to be made of viscous ink and dark bioluminescence. They have no bones; their form is sustained only by the surface tension of magic and willpower. They never touch the ground, floating a few centimetres above it at all times.

Blood does not flow within them , they are the blood. When wounded, they do not bleed: they lose cohesion, evaporating into curls of sweet-smelling smoke. Their two evolutionary paths diverge on a fundamental philosophical axis: those who find purpose through spiritual guidance move toward becoming anchors of the soul, while those drawn to the wisdom of the natural world become masters of organic alchemy and life itself.
