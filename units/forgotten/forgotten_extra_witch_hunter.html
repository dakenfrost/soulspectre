Witch Hunter

Tier 3 · Hostile Unit

Those Who Study the Root

They are not soldiers. They are specialists. Where the regular imperial army brings numbers and steel, Witch Hunters bring knowledge — specifically, knowledge of how to undo what Malicott has built. They spend years studying the nature of the pact: the amber compounds, the sap-to-blood transition, the precise points in the root network where a severing cut causes the most damage.

They carry blessed blades — not blessed in the religious sense, but treated with compounds that disrupt organic magic at a molecular level. They carry amber-breaking tools: small, precise, and expensive. They work methodically, the way a surgeon works, and they regard the Forgotten not as enemies to be defeated but as problems to be dissolved.

The Forgotten find them deeply unsettling. Not because they are powerful — they are — but because they are calm. They have no hatred. They have a method.
