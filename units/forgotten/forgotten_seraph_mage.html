Seraph Mage

Tier 5 , Champion of the Araldi

The Architecture of the Sky

The Seraph Mage is not "seraph" in the theological sense , she is not a messenger. The name refers to a classification of an older magic system, one that predated organized religion's attempt to claim divine hierarchy as its vocabulary. In that system, seraph meant the point at which elemental convergence achieves structural integrity. A seraph-class mage was not someone who channeled an element. She was someone who had become the meeting point between elements, and could shape that intersection deliberately.

She died three hundred years ago in a city whose name has been administratively removed from all surviving documents. The cause was not violence but physics: she attempted to merge two incompatible resonance fields as an act of creation, and the merger was successful in ways she did not survive. Malicott found what remained and recognized something that had not yet finished working.

The Seraph Mage does not think of herself as powerful. She thinks of herself as accurate. The distinction matters. Power implies effort; accuracy implies understanding. She has stopped expending effort on resistance that could simply be bypassed. Everything she channels goes through the substrate of reality directly , clean, fast, and without negotiating for right of way.

Space Blade

Base
A coherent blade of Pure and Light energy that attacks the structural layer of reality rather than the surface of the target. Deals full ATK in combined damage with full resistance bypass , no elemental resistance, armor, or barrier reduces this damage. The mathematics of the hit are impeccably clean.

Celestial Conclave

Active
The Seraph Mage opens a resonance field at the intersection of her light and air elements, releasing a convergence wave that strikes all enemies for combined Light + Air damage. All enemies hit suffer -20 Morale immediately. Units at 0 Morale receive a Stagger state lasting 1 turn, preventing them from using Active or Ultimate skills.

Transcendence

Passive
The Seraph Mage has transcended the ordinary boundaries between unit death and elemental power. She gains +8% ATK for every allied unit that has fallen during the current battle, with no maximum. Additionally, the Seraph Mage's own status immunity means all incoming debuffs are deflected , they do not apply to her under any circumstances.

Solar Order

Ultimate
Once per battle. The Seraph Mage restructures the formation's elemental output , for 2 full turns, all allied attacks deal an additional +30% of their base ATK as bonus Pure damage, which fully ignores resistances. All allied cooldowns are reduced by 1 turn when this is activated. This is not a battle cry. This is engineering applied to violence.
