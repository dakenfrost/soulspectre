Elf Alchemist

Tier 4 , Nature Path

Light That Does Not Burn

The Elf Alchemist remembers the cathedral. Not its name, not its city , but the quality of the light inside it: amber, warm, falling through ancient stone in columns so thick they felt solid. She remembers holding a crystal up to that light for the first time and feeling something answer back from within it.

What she carries now is a fragment of that first stone. She has never put it down. In the Economy of Merit she is classified as a transmuter , someone who changes the nature of what already exists rather than creating or destroying. She does not convert damage into nothing. She redirects it, filters it, refines it into something the body can absorb and use.

Her eyes hold the same quality as the crystal: green at the edges, gold at the center, lit from somewhere that is not quite here. She smiles often. It is the smile of someone who has learned to find the cathedral in every room they enter.
