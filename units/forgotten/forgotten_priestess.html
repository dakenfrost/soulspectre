Priestess

Officer , Shadows of the Sap

The Pain Is Only Information

The Priestess understands something the rest of the battlefield does not: that every wound is not a loss but a request for resources. In the Economy of Merit, the Linfa operate by the same principle as the Empire's broader society , nothing is wasted, everything can be redistributed, and grief without action is a luxury no one can afford.

Those who become Priestesses in life were often caretakers of unmanageable scope: healers during plagues, leaders during famines, counselors of the broken. Death did not stop the reflex. Malicott's sap simply gave them back the hands for it.

Their form is the most defined of the Shadows of the Sap branch , a barely visible skeleton suspended within a luminous viscous cloud that moves like thought rather than physics. They address wounds across the battlefield by pointing. They address death by not accepting it.

Malicott's Blessing

Base
The Priestess channels Malicott's grace outward, simultaneously dealing minor Light damage to nearby enemies and healing the most wounded ally in range for 10% of their maximum HP. A reminder that protection and aggression are the same motion.

Descending Grace

Active
A wave of golden-dark sap unfurls across the entire formation. Every allied unit is healed for 18% of their maximum HP. Units below 30% HP receive double the effect. The Priestess directs the triage , she will not let the Empire bleed out one soldier at a time.

Sacred Aura

Passive
The Priestess's holy aura amplifies every healing action in the formation. All heals applied to allied units are +20% more effective. Rally effects resist dispel. The Empire's supply line runs through the living will of the Priestess , she is the IV line of the entire squad.

Awakening

Ultimate
The Priestess reaches into the space between the living and the dead and pulls an ally back. Resurrects one fallen allied unit with 50% HP, removes all negative status effects from the resurrected unit, and grants them +20% Damage for 2 turns. The dead were borrowed , Risveglio is the return.
