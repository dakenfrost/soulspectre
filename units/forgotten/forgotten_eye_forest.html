Eye of the Forest

Tier 5 , Champion of the Sentinelle

What the Forest Records

The forests do not forget. The Empire's forests least of all , they are Malicott's original domain, places where the sap remembers every wound and every healing, where the root systems carry information older than the settlements built above them. The Eye of the Forest is not a title she chose. It is a function she fulfills: the forest needed an instrument for its observation, and she is what was available and willing.

She died doing what she had done her whole life , watching. She was a forest warden, a protector of boundaries, someone who spent decades at the observation points of the old empire knowing where every disturbance originated before it had produced consequences visible to anyone else. She was very good at this. She is extraordinarily good at this in death, because in undeath the forest's own vision layers on top of hers, and what she sees through both sets of eyes simultaneously is something closer to the total picture than any living creature normally accesses.

She does not describe this as power. She describes it as clarity. The battlefield, from where she stands, has no hidden positions. There are no invisible units, no obscured lines of sight, no strategic ambiguities. There is only what is there , and the vast majority of what is there can be reached from where she stands, at range, with absolute precision.

Predator's Vision

Base
The Eye of the Forest fires from perfect positional awareness , her shot cannot miss under any circumstances. Physical ranged damage equal to full ATK bypasses all Dodge, Evasion, and accuracy modifier effects. The target's ability to avoid it is irrelevant. The forest saw where it was going before the arrow left the bow.

Total Surveillance

Active
The Eye of the Forest extends forest-vision across the entire battlefield. All enemies are Revealed for 2 turns , Revealed enemies cannot use Dodge or Evasion, and take +20% increased damage from all sources. Stealth, partial shadow states, and positioning concealment are all negated. The forest has no concept of hiding.

Hunting Net

Passive
The Eye of the Forest's observation network benefits the entire formation. All allied units gain a permanent +10 Accuracy bonus from her battlefield intelligence. For each enemy currently afflicted by Exposed, Marked, or Revealed status, the Eye of the Forest gains +12% ATK , the more the field is known, the more precisely she can exploit it.

Forest Verdict

Ultimate
Once per battle. The Eye of the Forest delivers a single forest-sanctioned shot , a guaranteed critical strike dealing 200% ATK in Physical + Earth damage that ignores all resistances and armor. If the target survives below 25% HP, they are Stunned for 2 turns and cannot be healed during that period. The entire enemy formation suffers -30 Morale simultaneously. The forest has issued its verdict.
