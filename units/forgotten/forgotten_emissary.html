Emissary

Tier 5 Champion , Shadows of the Sap

The Living Covenant

An Emissary is what happens when a Linfa soul fully integrates all dichotomies and stops experiencing them as tension. She is simultaneously Malicott's most complete manifestation and the most individually present unit in the entire Shadows of the Sap branch. Where lesser units are still becoming, the Emissary has arrived , not at transcendence, but at perfect function.

She negotiates between life and death the way an experienced diplomat negotiates between two states that have been at war for a century: with total familiarity with both positions, zero illusions about either, and an absolute refusal to let the impasse continue. She does not mourn the fallen. She retrieves them and brings them back to work.

Her voice carries the specific timbre of sap-harmonics , each word is simultaneously spoken and felt as a vibration in the bones. Enemies who have heard her speak describe it as hearing the forest itself issue a sentence. Allies describe it differently: they say it sounds like someone who genuinely believes that the outcome is not in question. Only the timeline.

Voice of Malicott

Base
The Emissary speaks Malicott's truth across the battlefield. All enemies suffer Mind + Light damage and have their Morale reduced by 15. Simultaneously, all allied units are healed for 8% of their maximum HP. One sentence. Two audiences. Neither gets to look away.

Diplomatic Immunity

Active
The Emissary invokes a covenant that the battlefield itself recognizes. For 1 full turn, all allied units cannot be reduced below 1 HP and are immune to all negative status effects. The effects of ongoing damage still accumulate, but the floor is established. You may finish your strike , the result will not be what you intended.

Life Pact

Ultimate , Once per Battle
The Emissary draws on the full weight of Malicott's covenant and issues a single absolute command to the boundary between living and dead. Every fallen allied unit is resurrected with 25% of their maximum HP, all negative status effects removed. Every living allied unit is healed for 30% of their maximum HP. The battle resets , but the enemy does not. This is not a miracle. It is an outcome that was always going to happen.
