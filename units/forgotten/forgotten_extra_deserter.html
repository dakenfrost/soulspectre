Deserter

Tier 1 · Hostile Unit

Fugitives of the Empire

When the Crimson Campaign reached its most brutal phase, not all soldiers obeyed their orders. Some broke — not from cowardice, but from the accumulated weight of what they had been asked to burn. They fled into the wilderness still wearing the empire's colors, because they had nowhere else to go.

Now they haunt the forest edge in small groups, too afraid of the Forgotten to go deeper and too compromised to return to the cities. They have become something that belongs to neither world: dangerous because they are desperate, and desperate because they are ashamed. They attack supply lines and isolated parties indiscriminately, driven by hunger and the blunt logic of survival.

The Forgotten recognize what they are. That recognition does not make them less of a threat.
