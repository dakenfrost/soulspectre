The Basics

The Capital

Your starting base. Produces resources each turn. Defended by your Leader. Its loss results in a Game Over.

The Party

Requires an Officer as leader (customizable name). Parties consist of the leader plus up to 5 additional members.

The Combat System

Turn-based party vs party. Highest initiative acts first. Battle ends when one party is totally annihilated.

Provinces

Control cities to harvest resources. Many points are guarded and require military victory to acquire.

Awakening

Clearing specific dungeons increases Awakening level. Each level grants one out of three unique passive skills.

Victory

Achieve specific campaign goals or eliminate all other factions by destroying their Capitals.
