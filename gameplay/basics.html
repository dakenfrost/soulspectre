The Basics

The Capital

Your starting base. It produces a base amount of resources each turn and allows you to build structures and form parties. The capital is defended by your Leader, making it extremely hard to conquer, but its loss results in a Game Over.

The Party

Each party requires an Officer as leader, whom you can name as you wish. A party can be composed of just the leader or include up to 5 additional members for a total of 6 units.

The Combat System

Battles are turn-based encounters between two parties. The unit with the highest Initiative acts first and selects a skill. If initiative is tied, the order is random. The battle ends when one party is totally annihilated.

Provinces

Each province has its own city. Controlling it allows you to harvest additional resources. Be aware: some resources may be guarded by enemies that must be defeated before you can acquire the assets.

Awakening

Clearing specific dungeons will increase the Awakening level of your officers. Each level lets you select one of three passive skills, providing bonuses such as increased attack or additional elemental damage.

Victory

Campaigns often have specific goals, but in normal mode, a faction wins once all other factions are defeated and their capitals are destroyed.
