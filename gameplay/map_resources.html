Map & Resources

World Generation

Map generation depends on the active game mode. Campaigns feature specific, pre-designed maps for each scenario, while other modes utilize randomly generated maps.

Near the capital city, you will find low-level points of interest to level up your parties. As you venture further across the map, you will face progressively more challenging encounters.

Economy & Influence

There are three kinds of resources:

  • Gold - The primary currency used for most transactions.
  • Mana - Required to cast spells and to recruit or build specific units and structures.
  • Platinum - An extremely rare resource used to create Champion Units.

The map features different tiers of cities, each projecting influence over the surrounding zone (marked by a specific color). The faction controlling a city receives a specific amount of resources each turn, calculated based on the structures built and the resource nodes secured within its zone of influence.

Points of Interest

There are several types of points of interest scattered across the map. These are often guarded by hostile NPC parties; defeating higher-difficulty parties yields greater resource rewards.

Below is the list of points of interest:

  • Gold Mine - Generates Gold for the controlling city.
  • Mana Shrine - Generates Mana for the controlling city.
  • Platinum Mine - Generates Platinum for the controlling city (usually heavily guarded for a very low yield).
  • Wandering Hostiles - Defeating them grants random rewards.
  • Merchant Shop - Allows buying and selling of items.
  • Dungeon - Features multiple floors. Depending on the difficulty, clearing it rewards items and/or Awakening progression.
  • Raid - Highly challenging encounters for elite parties, yielding epic rewards and Awakening progression.
  • Magic Tower - Sells random spells in exchange for Mana.
  • Blessing Shrine - Provides combat buffs to allied units operating within its vicinity.
  • Gate of Chaos - Similar to a random encounter, but continuously respawns enemies every X turns until closed.
  • Gate of Hell - Similar to a Raid, but respawns elite enemies every X turns until sealed.
  • Quest Node - Objectives required to advance the current scenario.
