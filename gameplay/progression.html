Awakening System

Faction-Wide Mastery

Your journey begins at Awakening Level 0. Unlike standard leveling, Awakening is a global progression system that represents your faction's growing connection to the spectral realm.

Clearing dungeons, conquering ancient ruins, or defeating formidable legendary opponents will grant Awakening Experience. When the faction levels up, all Officers—current and future—benefit from the unlocked potential simultaneously.

The Choice of Power

Each time your Awakening Level increases, you are granted a choice of three random perks. These perks provide permanent passive attribute bonuses to your Officers, allowing you to specialize your military force.

Perks are divided into five rarity tiers, each offering progressively stronger bonuses:

Milestones & Syncing

Every 5 Levels, the standard perk selection is replaced by Major Perks. These yellow-tier upgrades grant game-changing abilities, such as increased Initiative, extra attacks, or powerful passive modifiers for Officer skills.

Level Synchronization

An Officer's Awakening potential is linked to their combat experience. An Officer can access Awakening levels up to Hero Level × 5.

Example: A Level 2 Hero can utilize up to 10 Awakening levels. If your faction has reached Level 13, that hero will remain capped at 10 until they reach Level 3, at which point the remaining 3 levels will unlock automatically.

The maximum Awakening Level is currently capped at 30.
