Officers & Parties

Leadership & Party Dynamics

Officers serve as the primary commanders of your active parties. Each Officer can lead a base of 3 to 5 units, scaling entirely on their Leadership stat. A faction is strictly limited to a maximum of 5 active Officers at any given time.

Officers can equip gear and unlock additional equipment slots as they progress. This gear enhances base statistics, grants specific elemental protections, or unlocks unique tactical skills.

Casualties and Resurrection

If an Officer falls in battle, their surviving party will suffer a 50% movement speed penalty on the global map until the commander is resurrected. Fallen Officers can be revived at any controlled City, provided at least one member of their party survived the encounter. If the entire party is annihilated, all members—including the Officer—are permanently lost.

Progression & Stat Growth

Officers and units incapable of further evolution (either due to missing infrastructure or having reached their maximum tier) experience fixed stat growth upon leveling up. They gain Health Points (HP) and Attack equal to 50% of their base Tier 1 stats.

Example: An Officer with a base of 150 HP and 50 Attack will gain 75 HP and 25 Attack per level. Note that high-tier units calculate this bonus based strictly on their original Tier 1 form (e.g., a Tier 4 unit whose Tier 1 form had 25 Attack will gain 13 Attack per level, rounded up).

Officer Level-Up Perks

Upon leveling up, an Officer may select one of the following tactical upgrades:

  • +1 Leadership Capacity
  • +1 Equipment Slot (if slots are still locked)
  • +1 Elemental Protection (element of your choice)
Note: In addition to standard level-up perks, Officers are also affected by the global Awakening progression system. Consult the Awakening doctrine for further parameters.

Evolution & Champions

When a unit gains enough experience to evolve into a higher tier, a medal icon will appear on the post-battle screen, allowing you to select its next evolutionary path. Warning: Any experience gained beyond the evolution threshold is permanently lost. You must upgrade them as soon as possible to avoid data waste.

Champion Units

Champion Units are elite assets strictly capped at 1 for each combat category: Melee, Ranged, Support, Magic, and Hybrid. A new Champion in a specific category can only be forged if the current one has been permanently killed (which requires the total annihilation of their assigned party).
