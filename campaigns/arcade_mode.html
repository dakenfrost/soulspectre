Arcade Protocol

The Endless Simulation

Welcome to the Gauntlet. The Arcade Protocol is a procedurally generated theater of war stripped of politics and alliances. Here, you will select your allegiance and face a gauntlet of randomized factions in a relentless escalation of force. Survival is not guaranteed.

I

Tactical Escalation

Combat is divided into five escalating tiers. In Tier 1, you are restricted to basic infantry and low-level spells. To advance, you must shatter the enemy vanguard and execute their commanding Hero—a powerful entity operating one tier above your current limitations.

II

The Interrogations

Violence brings questions, not peace. Following the execution of an enemy commander, the simulation will pause. You will be asked a question. Your answers will not affect the immediate battle, but they will silently shape the anomaly waiting at the end of the simulation. Choose your philosophy carefully.

III

The Realm of Shadows

Should you survive the five mortal tiers, the simulation collapses into the Realm of Shadows. Here, foreign armies cease to exist. You will face the darkest reflection of your own allegiance—perfect, merciless doppelgangers of your own heroes and units. Your greatest tactical strengths will be weaponized against you.

IV

The Soul Spectre

If the shadows are purged, only the Architect remains. The Soul Spectre is the final anomaly—an entity born from the desperate psychological need to escape reality. It wields the very concepts you forged during the Interrogations. To win, you must defeat the manifestation of your own escapism.
